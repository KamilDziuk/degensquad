Degen Squad is an ERC-20 token built on the Degen Chain. Our mission is to create a Degen community and promote knowledge about Web3 technology. A key element of Degen Squad is focusing on building a strong and active community in Farcaster, emphasizing good humor, knowledge, and cooperation. As Degen Squad, we work on providing access to educational resources about blockchain technology, smart contracts, and DeFi, encouraging users to engage more deeply with the Web3 space. Degen Squad aims to create an environment where everyone can learn and develop in the Web3 space, supporting the growth of a strong community based on education and good humor.
