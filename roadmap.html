Roadmap


First Epoch - The Forest

Welcome to the first epoch of our adventure in the forest. This phase is the cornerstone for Degen Squad, where our goal is to plant the seeds of our community, which will grow into a mighty forest full of diversity and innovation.

Here's what we intend to do to make our vision a reality:

Building Community
Creating social channels: We will launch platforms such as Telegram, Twitter, YouTube to serve as our channels for promotion and communication.

Engaging Early Users
We invite enthusiasts, content creators, and everyone who wants to contribute to our mission.

Marketing Activities
Starting campaigns aimed at promoting Degen Squad and building brand awareness.

Publications and Promotional Materials We will use blogs, web3 article sites for the promotion of Degen Squad.

Transparency and Communication
We will keep our community informed about progress, planned actions, and any changes in the project. Transparency is key, so we commit to regularly sharing updates and future plans.

Encouraging Engagement
Our community is the heart of Degen Squad, therefore we encourage everyone to actively participate in the community and receive $DGNQ tips.
